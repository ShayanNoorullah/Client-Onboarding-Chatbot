AWESOME TECHNOLOGIES INC. Privacy Policy

AWESOME TECHNOLOGIES INC (ATI) and its website www.awesometechinc.com respect your privacy. This Privacy Statement informs you of our privacy practices and of the choices you can make about the way your information is collected online and how that information is used.

1. SITES COVERED BY THIS PRIVACY STATEMENT
ATI is a global organization with legal entities and multiple websites that cross borders. This Privacy Statement applies to all ATI-owned websites and domains and the websites and domains of our wholly owned subsidiaries.

2. COLLECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION
ATI collects, exports, and uses personal information with appropriate notice and consent where required. For example, when you order products or services, request information, subscribe to marketing or support materials, register products, enter contests or surveys, or apply for employment.

The types of personal information may include: name, address, phone number, email, financial data such as credit card information, user IDs and passwords, billing and transaction details, product preferences, and employment and educational background.

ATI may also collect information automatically through cookies, web beacons, embedded web links, and information gathering tools. This may include browser type, IP address, clickstream behavior, referring pages, and access times.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
ATI uses collected information to process transactions, provide support and services, communicate about products and updates, deliver promotional offers, personalize website content, and conduct market research. Credit card information is used only for payment processing and fraud prevention.

4. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION
ATI does not sell, rent, or lease personal information to third parties. We may share personal information with service providers (payment processors, shipping agents, email providers), marketing partners (where you opt in), and ATI-owned global entities. We may also disclose personal information if required by law, subpoena, court order, or to protect ATI's rights and safety.

5. COMMUNICATIONS AND SMS USAGE
We use SMS solely to send one-time passcodes (OTP) for login authentication and security verification. Messages are transactional, not promotional. Mobile numbers collected for authentication are not sold, rented, or shared for marketing purposes.

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
ATI does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 and does not target its website to children under 13.

7. YOUR CHOICES AND PRIVACY PREFERENCES
You may opt out of marketing communications by clicking the unsubscribe link in emails, returning to the registration page, or contacting the ATI Privacy Office. Transactional or service-related communications cannot be opted out of if required for service delivery.

8. ACCESS TO AND ACCURACY OF YOUR INFORMATION
You may request access to, correction, anonymization, or deletion of your personal information where applicable. Identity verification may be required before granting access.

9. KEEPING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION SECURE
ATI uses physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to protect your data. Sensitive data such as credit card information is protected using encryption technologies such as SSL (Secure Socket Layer).

If this Privacy Statement changes, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated revision date.
