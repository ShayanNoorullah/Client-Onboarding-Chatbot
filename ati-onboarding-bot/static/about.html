About Client Onboarding Agent

Awesome Technologies Inc.

The Client Onboarding Agent helps prospective clients describe their project needs through a guided conversation. We collect requirements, accept file uploads, and generate a structured project brief for ATI advisors to review.

Your data is used only to prepare your brief and is never sold to third parties. You may request deletion at any time.

How it works

1. Review privacy and consent

2. Share your name and project goals

3. Chat naturally — upload files when helpful

4. Receive a structured brief for ATI advisors within 3 business days
